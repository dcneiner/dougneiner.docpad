Speaking

If you spend any time around me at all, you will quickly realize I enjoy talking. Once you know that, its not too surprising to find out I also enjoy speaking to audiences large and small. I feel the audience gets the most benefit when I am talking about topics I am passionate about, including jQuery, Amplify JS, and Design.

Over the past few years I have had the pleasure of presenting at a number of conferences across America and even one in London:

  • jQuery San Francisco Bay Area Conference (2010, 2011)
  • jQuery Boston Conference (2010, 2011)
  • RIA Unleashed (2010, 2011)
  • Front End Design Conference (2011)
  • jQuery UK Conference (2012)

If you are interested in having me speak at your conference, please drop me a line.
